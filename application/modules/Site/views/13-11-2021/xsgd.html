STRAITSx
Tapping into smart contracts to reshape the FinTech infrastructure
Our secure-by design Scilla is the ideal smart contract language to support a core pillar of an 'Open Finance' ecosystem - stablecoins.

Starting with powering XSGD, the first Singapore dollar-pegged stablecoin built by Xfers, we are forging the way towards a secure, efficient and interoperable digital currency ecosystem in Asia Pacific.
XSGD: Digital Assets Liquidity
for Southeast Asia





Southeast Asian payments provider Xfers shared a goal similar to ours: to make finance in the region more accountable, accessible, and frictionless. With the inception of StraitsX - a pilot project to bring in reliable, regulated stablecoins into the region - we knew we had something special to work with. The first part of this project was building the XSGD, a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to a reserve of Singapore dollars, which immediately brought real-world utility to their wide customer base.

Zilliqa's smart contracts are the backbone of this infrastructure. They act as a solid security layer to efficiently manage peer-to-peer payments and cross-border transactions.

The mainstream use of digital currency marks a turning point in the financial world. Soon, we envision that the use of DLT will open up access to trading and financial services like never before.

Built on Zilliqa's secure platform

The XSGD smart contract and the stablecoin is deployed onto the Zilliqa blockchain. This essentially means that all transaction fees are denominated in our native token $ZIL.
Secure
The Zilliqa platform is integrated with risk management and AML compliance tools by Elliptic, providing an additional layer of security to all tokens built on $ZIL - including XSGD.
Scalable
Zilliqa's sharded platform will ensure that as the XSGD continues to gain adopters, they can process higher transaction volumes swiftly.
Transparent
The on-chain transaction monitoring feature enables customers to monitor XSGD transactions and view all information through the Elliptic interface.
